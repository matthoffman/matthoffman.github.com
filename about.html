about the title

I'll be honest — I was a little hesitant naming it "matt thinks so," in case the tongue-in-cheek-ness didn't convey. There's an implied "...and we all know what THAT's worth" at the end of the blog's title — not to be confused with "...and therefore, you should believe it" or "...and thus it shall be so!" Blogging might have some degree of built-in hubris to it, but I draw the line at claiming omniscience.
